Welcome to Coppers Odds Shoe Raffle Service Program

 

This is the help guide for users new to the program. The section is divided into six sections.

• Login/Registering

• Dashboard

• Shoes

• Payments

• My Account

• About Us

Section #1: Login/Registering

Logging into Coppers Odds is simple and easy. In the text field next to the user icon, please type in your username. In the text field next to the lock icon, please type in your password. Then, click the "Login" button. If your password or username is typed incorrectly, Coppers Odds will not be able to identify the account. 

If you are a new user for Coppers Odds, you may click on the "Register" button. You will be prompted to a new window where you have to enter in your username, email, and password. Please enter the required fields, then click "Sign Up", to sign up for Coppers Odds. If you already have an existing account, click "Login". Else, if you want to close the application, click on "Close". After signing up, please login with the correct credentials.

If you forgot your password, you can reset it very easily. On the bottom left corner, click on the text that says "Reset Password". Then, you will be prompted to enter the email that you have associated with your Coppers Odds account. If you don't type in the correct email, Coppers Odds won't be able to reset your account. After typing the correct email, a verification code will be sent to your email. Please enter that code for Coppers Odds. Then, you will be able to reset your old password, and finally, log back into your Coppers Odds account.

 

Section #2: Dashboard

The dashboard shows many data and stats about your Coppers Odds account. You will be able to see your raffle ticket prize count, total raffle tickets bought, trending shoes, the current percentage of winning, your internet status, and my account. Additionally, you can click on the Refresh Button to refresh, Logout Button to logout, and View Purchases Button to view all purchases.

 

Section #3: Shoes:

Here, you will be prompted to a new window that shows all the currently available shoes. It also shows the number of tickets sold, tickets bought, and your chance of winning. Click on any of the shoe pictures to get more details.

Then, you will be lead to a window that shows all the colors, sizes, and other information. Click on "Buy Raffle Tickets Here", to buy raffle tickets. 

Then, it shows data about the current total raffle tickets, the number of raffle tickets you bought, and your percent of winning. Click on "Buy Tickets" to buy your tickets.

Finally, please enter your email and the number of tickets to buy. When you click the buy button, you will be prompted to a Paypal Purchase. Complete the purchase to get your reward.

 

Section #4: Payments

Here you will be able to give feedback, get help, and open all purchases. When you click the "Open Purchases Securely in a New Window", a new window will show up with a table of all the purchases you have made.

 

Section #5: My Account

Here you will be able to access your account details and reset some of them if necessary. You will be able to reset your username, first name, last name, password, and biography if needed. Click save to save your account details, click Main Menu for the main menu, and click close to close the window.

 

Section #6: About Us

Clicking on this makes a new window popup talking about the developers and Coppers Odds.

 

Conclusion

This wraps up the help section for Coppers Odds. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact Coppers Odds at coppersoddss@gmail.com. Thank you!
